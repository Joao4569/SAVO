Who are we?

The Swiss Association of Veterinary Ophthalmologists (SAVO) is a group of veterinarians who have a special interest in ophthalmology. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality of eye care for your pet. Our goal is to provide the best possible care for your pet and to help you understand the importance of good eye health.

Our members are all board-certified veterinary ophthalmologists who have completed advanced training in the diagnosis and treatment of eye diseases in animals. We work closely with your veterinarian to provide the best possible care for your pet.

We are committed to providing the highest quality of care for your pet and to helping you understand the importance of good eye health. We are here to help you and your pet with any eye problems that you may have.

Why do animals need specialized eye veterinarians?

Animals, like humans, can suffer from a variety of eye problems that require specialized care. Veterinary ophthalmologists are trained to diagnose and treat a wide range of eye diseases in animals. They have the knowledge and expertise to provide the best possible care for your pet's eyes.
